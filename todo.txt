Shopkeepers run your POS (in-store + online via Woo/Shopify/Daraz sync), manage orders, couriers, basic accounting
Main vendors/suppliers list their catalog centrally
Any shopkeeper can pull a vendor's products into their own store/website without holding inventory
When a customer orders, the shopkeeper doesn't pre-buy — the order triggers a purchase from the vendor (essentially dropshipping infrastructure, vendor-to-shopkeeper-to-end-customer)
Vendor's stock levels sync in real time across every shopkeeper/website using that product

That's effectively: POS/Retail Management + Multi-channel sync + B2B2C Dropship Marketplace + Order/Courier orchestration, all in one SaaS.
